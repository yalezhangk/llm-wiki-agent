## Summary
This presentation introduces [[GHA]] as a [[施耐德电气]] gas-insulated medium-voltage switchgear platform up to 40.5kV with a vacuum circuit-breaker, single-busbar and double-busbar variants, sealed gas compartments, and a user-oriented operating interface. Its distinctive technical themes are [[BLink]] busbar connection without site gas handling, [[IDIS]] gas-density indication, [[ILIS]] internal-arc detection and turn-off, cable connection flexibility, seismic qualification, and [[内部电弧防护]].

## Key Claims
- [[GHA]] is positioned as [[气体绝缘开关柜]] with a vacuum circuit-breaker up to 40.5kV, covering both single-busbar and double-busbar switchgear arrangements.
- The deck repeatedly frames the product around sealed-for-life gas compartments, touch-proof construction, and protection from dust, humidity, small animals, UV/light, salt, and aggressive gases.
- [[BLink]] allows switchgear panels to be erected, extended, disassembled, or moved within a switchboard row without gas handling outside the manufacturer's site.
- [[BLink]] uses single-pole silicone-rubber insulation for busbar connections, is earthed against contact voltage, and is type-tested at 40.5kV / BIL 185kV.
- The B-link screw-type contact is stated as type-tested at 40kA for 3s and 104kA peak current.
- Gas compartments are described as a sealed pressure system according to IEC 62271-1, with leakage rate `<< 0.1 % p.a.` and gas removal by commercial valve at end of service life for recycling.
- [[IDIS]] provides per-compartment gas-density indication through temperature-compensated gas density switches, two alarm levels, traffic-light display, remote signaling, group signal, and self-monitoring.
- Cable systems include outer-cone connections up to and including 36kV / BIL 170kV, optional inner-cone connections up to 40.5kV, and fully insulated busbar systems up to 2500A via inner-cone plug-in systems.
- [[ILIS]] detects and analyzes internal arc faults, with a release logic combining protection-relay current and internal-arc detection above `>= 1 kA`; the deck states a total turn-off time of approximately 100ms.
- The GHA option set includes IAC AFLR up to 40kA for 1s, upward/backward pressure relief, an optional exhaust duct, and seismic qualification including UBC seismic zone 4 references.
