## Summary
This 2018 catalog is the formal product catalog for [[GHA]], a [[施耐德电气]] gas-insulated medium-voltage switchgear platform up to 40.5kV. It confirms the general GHA ratings, IEC single-busbar and double-busbar configurations, GHA Rail and ANSI application branches, [[BLink]], [[IDIS]], [[IVIS]], cable connection options, [[EcoStruxure]] ready solutions, environmental handling of SF6, and [[内部电弧防护]].

## Key Claims
- [[GHA]] is gas-insulated switchgear and controlgear with vacuum circuit-breaker for public and industrial medium-voltage distribution systems up to 40.5kV.
- General IEC ratings cover 12/17.5/24/36/38/40.5kV, rated lightning impulse withstand voltage 75/95/125/170/170/185kV, rated short-time current 40kA, rated peak withstand current 100kA, rated busbar current 2500A with higher values up to 4000A on request, and rated branch current up to 2500A.
- Internal Arc Classification is listed as 40kA/1s; all cubicles are described as IAC AFL 1s as standard, with IAC AFLR available when rear access is required.
- GHA modules are organized around single or double busbar, outer or inner cone cable connection, and IAC AFL or AFLR options.
- IEC single-busbar and double-busbar functional overviews both cover up to 40.5kV and up to 40kA, with feeder currents up to 2500A.
- GHA Rail is a railway-derived single-busbar branch for one-pole or two-pole configurations, with nominal voltage 25/25-0-25kV, rated voltage 27.5/27.5-0-27.5kV, busbar/feeders up to 2000A, and up to 2500A / 31.5kA for one-pole configuration.
- GHA ANSI is described as UL and CSA certified for ANSI applications, with ratings up to 38kV, 2500A and 40kA.
- Gas-filled compartments are sealed pressure systems according to IEC/EN 62271-1, made of chromium-nickel steel, leakproof across service life, and delivered factory-filled with no gas handling needed for installation, extension, replacement, or dismantling under normal conditions.
- [[BLink]] connects adjacent GHA panel busbars without site gas work and is described as IP65 and maintenance-free; the catalog states its current-carrying connection was checked at 40kA for 3s and 100kA peak current.
- [[IDIS]] monitors autonomous gas-filled compartments through pressure sensors and displays gas status by green/yellow/red LEDs, with a common remote gas warning group signal.
- [[IVIS]] is an integrated voltage detection system for voltage presence, isolation verification, and phase comparison according to IEC/EN 61243-5.
- EcoStruxure ready content connects GHA with nearby control, Substation Monitoring Device, HMI, protection/metering/condition monitoring, Easergy P3/Sepam/MiCOM, VAMP arc mitigation, TH110 thermal monitoring, CL110 ambient monitoring, SCADA and cloud/mobile use cases.
- Environmental content states that GHA contains SF6 in sealed pressure vessels, supports closed SF6 circulation and recovery through self-sealing valves, and has a 90% potential recyclability ratio by weight excluding packaging.

## Contradictions
- The 2018 catalog refines the older GHA presentation by providing a complete general ratings table. Future GHA selection should prefer this catalog over the legacy PPT for ratings.
- The B-link peak-current wording differs by source: the older presentation states 104kA peak current, while this catalog states 100kA peak current. Treat this as a version/documentation difference and verify against project-specific ordering documents before engineering use.
- The catalog does not explicitly repeat the older presentation's [[ILIS]] name; it instead describes EcoStruxure-ready arc mitigation using Easergy P3 and VAMP-style arc detection. This is a scope difference rather than a confirmed contradiction.
