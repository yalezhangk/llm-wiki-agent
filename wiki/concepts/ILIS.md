## Summary
[[ILIS]] is the GHA internal-fault detection system for [[内部电弧防护]]. In the presentation it is framed as an option for detecting, analyzing, and turning off internal arc faults inside [[GHA]], complementing the switchgear's IAC AFLR withstand classification.

## Key Details
- ILIS is described as a system for detecting internal faults.
- The release logic combines a protection-relay current criterion with internal-arc detection.
- The discrimination threshold is stated as `>= 1 kA`.
- The total turn-off time is stated as approximately 100ms.
- The option is presented alongside GHA internal-arc classification and pressure-relief design.
