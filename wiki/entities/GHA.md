## Summary
[[GHA]] is a [[施耐德电气]] medium-voltage [[气体绝缘开关柜]] platform described as gas-insulated switchgear with a vacuum circuit-breaker up to 40.5kV. In the current wiki it complements [[GMA]] and [[GMAirSeT]] by extending the gas-insulated switchgear knowledge base into a higher-voltage, [[BLink]]-based product family with [[IDIS]] gas compartment monitoring, [[IVIS]] voltage detection, [[EcoStruxure]] ready monitoring, and internal-arc protection options.

## Key Details
- Product positioning: gas-insulated switchgear with vacuum circuit-breaker up to 40.5kV.
- System variants include IEC single-busbar and double-busbar arrangements, GHA Rail, and GHA ANSI.
- General IEC ratings in the 2018 catalog cover 12/17.5/24/36/38/40.5kV, rated short-time current 40kA, rated peak withstand current 100kA, rated busbar current 2500A with higher values up to 4000A on request, and branch current up to 2500A.
- GHA Rail is a railway-derived single-busbar branch with nominal voltage 25/25-0-25kV and rated voltage 27.5/27.5-0-27.5kV.
- GHA ANSI is UL and CSA certified for ANSI applications, with ratings up to 38kV, 2500A and 40kA.
- [[BLink]] is the core GHA busbar-connection feature, allowing erection, extension, panel disassembly, and panel movement without gas handling at site.
- Gas-filled compartments are presented as a sealed pressure system according to IEC/EN 62271-1, with maintenance-free behavior across service life; the older presentation states leakage rate `<< 0.1 % p.a.`.
- [[IDIS]] monitors each gas compartment with pressure sensors and green/yellow/red status LEDs, and can send a common remote gas warning group signal.
- [[IVIS]] provides integrated voltage detection for voltage presence, isolation verification, and phase comparison.
- Cable connection options include outer-cone connections up to 36kV and inner-cone connections up to 40.5kV.
- Fully insulated busbar-system connections are described up to 2500A via inner-cone plug-in systems.
- [[ILIS]] supports detection, analysis, and turn-off of internal arc faults with an approximately 100ms total turn-off time claim.
- Internal Arc Classification is listed as 40kA/1s; IAC AFL is standard in the catalog and IAC AFLR is available for rear access.
- [[EcoStruxure]] ready content connects GHA with nearby control, Substation Monitoring Device, HMI, protection/metering/condition monitoring, Easergy P3/Sepam/MiCOM, VAMP arc mitigation, TH110 and CL110 monitoring.
- Environmental content states that GHA contains SF6 in sealed pressure vessels, supports SF6 recovery through self-sealing valves, and has a 90% potential recyclability ratio by weight excluding packaging.
