## Summary
[[IVIS]] is the integrated voltage detection system described for [[GHA]], [[WSG]] and [[WIG]]. It supports zero-voltage verification, voltage-presence indication, and phase comparison in a [[气体绝缘开关柜]] cubicle equipped with capacitive voltage dividers.

## Key Details
- IVIS can be installed optionally in GHA switchgear cubicles with capacitive voltage dividers.
- The system is used to ascertain operating voltage presence, isolation from power supply, and phase comparison.
- The display indicates the capacitive decoupled voltage of each primary conductor phase.
- IVIS continuously checks the response conditions for unambiguous voltage-present/absent indication and repeat-test conditions according to IEC/EN 61243-5.
- The catalog describes IVIS as maintenance-free, weather-proof encapsulated, requiring no auxiliary voltage, and not requiring repeat tests using mobile equipment.
- The WI-G operation manual also places IVIS under the voltage detection system (VDS) chapter and states that it satisfies IEC 61243-5.
- WI-G describes IVIS as integrated with a display unit, requiring no external auxiliary supply, sealed as one unit, weather-resistant, maintenance-free, and capable of long-term live-state monitoring.
- WI-G also documents an alternative plug-in voltage detection system and notes that phase checking is required before first energization.
- The WS-G operation manual places IVIS under the voltage detection system chapter and states that it satisfies IEC 61243-5.
- The WS-G manual describes IVIS as an integrated voltage detection system with a display unit for checking the presence or absence of operating voltage.
- The WS-G manual states that IVIS needs no external auxiliary power, is sealed in one unit, weather-resistant, maintenance-free, and supports long-term live-state monitoring.
- The WS-G manual also describes a plug-in VDS according to IEC 61243-5 and requires phase checking before first energization.
